comeback note

Congratulations, aespa

Congratulations on your comeback, aespa. This LEMONADE era feels bright, fresh, colorful, and confident, while still keeping the strong identity that makes aespa stand out.

I love how the concept feels playful, glossy, and bold at the same time, like a new flavor without losing the energy that belongs to you. I really like this comeback, and I hope this era brings more love, more recognition, more beautiful stages, and more proud moments for aespa.

concept note

Why I love this era

What I love about this comeback is how it feels different, but still very aespa. The neon colors, the retro-futuristic mood, the sharp styling, and the bright LEMONADE feeling make this era look refreshing without becoming too soft.

It feels fun, confident, and experimental, but still powerful enough to remind everyone why aespa has always had their own world.

my hope

For this era

My hope for this era is simple: may LEMONADE become a beautiful chapter for aespa.

May every stage feel exciting, every result feel worth the hard work, and every moment bring more people to appreciate the color, talent, and presence that aespa keeps giving. I hope this comeback shines loudly and stays memorable for a long time.
